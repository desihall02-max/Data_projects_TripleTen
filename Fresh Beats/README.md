## üéµ Business Context: Fresh Beats

Fresh Beats operates a dual-purpose platform: it provides music streaming to enthusiasts and acts as a catalyst for up-and-coming musicians to gain visibility.

The company's unique revenue model is tied to **artist promotions**, where musicians pay for services to reach a broader audience. Therefore, optimizing promotional effectiveness and converting free users into highly engaged subscribers are critical strategic imperatives.

## üéØ Key Business Challenges Addressed

The analysis focused on providing data-backed recommendations to tackle three core strategic challenges identified by the business:

### 1. Artist Promotion Effectiveness

* **Challenge:** Evaluating current artist promotion strategies to ensure musicians receive maximum exposure and a strong return on investment (ROI).

* **Focus:** Identifying factors that significantly contribute to successful promotional campaigns.

### 2. User Engagement and Conversion

* **Challenge:** Enhancing user engagement on the platform and converting free users into paid subscribers.

* **Focus:** Understanding the specific drivers of user conversion and retention to ensure sustained revenue growth.

### 3. Genre Promotion for Emerging Artists

* **Challenge:** Improving the platform's content recommendation system, particularly for new artists.

* **Focus:** Tailoring genre-based recommendations based on user preferences and current music trends to create a more engaging user experience.

## üí° Key Findings & Actions (Based on Report Analysis)

The resulting stakeholder report provided actionable recommendations centered around genre trends and user tier behavior to directly address the business challenges:

| Business Challenge | Insight Derived | Recommended Action | 
 | ----- | ----- | ----- | 
| **Artist Promotion Effectiveness** | The **Pop genre** is on a downward trend, while **Electronic and Hip Hop** are trending upwards (Visual A). | To revitalize the Pop genre's promotional effectiveness, introduce Pop songs to active users of the currently trending genres (Electronic and Hip Hop) during their peak activity times. | 
| **User Engagement & Conversion** | **Paid users** exhibit higher activity (more music listened) and demonstrate steadier growth and better retention than free users (Visual B). | Utilize **testimonials and reviews** from the high-retention paid user base to influence and convert free users. | 
| **Engagement & Conversion (Combined)** | **Rock is the most popular genre for free users** (Visual C), representing a clear conversion opportunity. | Create a targeted promotional campaign focused on Rock music, offering a **new user sign-up bonus or deal** to convert highly engaged Rock-listening free users into paid subscribers. |
